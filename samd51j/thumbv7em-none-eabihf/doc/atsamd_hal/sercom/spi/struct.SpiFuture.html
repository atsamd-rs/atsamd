where R: AnyChannel<Status = ReadyFuture>, T: AnyChannel<Status = ReadyFuture>,

Attach RX and TX DMA channels to this SpiFuture. Its SpiBus implementation will use DMA to carry out its transactions. In Master mode, since even read SPI transaction necessarily involve a write to shift data in, Rx-only must take two DMA channels, just the same as if it were Duplex.
